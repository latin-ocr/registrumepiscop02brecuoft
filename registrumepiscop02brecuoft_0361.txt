Grift for life to Mr. Alexander Nory of the feu mails of the lands of Cortachie
with the pertinents lying within the diocese of Brechin and sheriffdom of
Forfar, formerly granted by the predecessore of the Lord of Brechyn to three
chaplainries of the choir of Brechin and six bairns to be sustained at the
school to sing in the said choir, with the houses and yards belonging to the
saids chaplainries and formerly belonging to Johnne sometime Earl of Mar,
as undoubted patron thereof, and now to the King by reason of escheat

through a process of forfeiture of which the said Earl was convicted in Pax-lia-
ment. Dated at Falkland 9th September 1584.
